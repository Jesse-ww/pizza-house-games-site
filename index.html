About Pizza House

Serving up fun handmade games.

I started Pizza House with the intention of creating player focused games. Too many games are releasing with sights set on profit rather than fun. I believe that if you design for fun, and player enjoyment, the rest will follow.
The studio might be fresh out of the oven, but I’ve been making games for 10 years. It’s time to start implementing all the knowledge that I’ve gained from trial and error.
