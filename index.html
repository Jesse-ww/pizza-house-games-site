>Why Pizza House?

Pizza House was started to create and share games that my friends and I would enjoy playing.

The AAA industry has shifted, and with every new release players are treated more like ATMs. Every year I find fewer games that I want to play and even fewer that deliver on their promises.

I want games to be about fun again. I miss going to the store, picking out a game for my Gameboy and getting to just play. No day one DLC or patches, no game breaking bugs, just fun.

Games, for the most part, were about art, entertainment, and wonder. I think we've lost that. But I also think we can get it back.

That's why Pizza House. I make games for players, for the art, for fun, for the wonder they bring, and for the inspration they can give. For people to enjoy.
