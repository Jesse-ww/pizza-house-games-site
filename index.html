Pizza what?

I started Pizza House to create player focused games. Too many games are released with sights set on profit rather than fun.

I believe that if you design for fun, and player enjoyment, the rest will follow.

The studio might be fresh out of the oven, but I’ve been making games as a hobby for 10 years. It’s time to start implementing all the knowledge that I’ve gained from trial and error.
